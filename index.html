How to get the cleanest results.
• DS-160: use the full Application printout (the multi-page PDF from the review screen), not the one-page confirmation — only the full printout carries every answer.
• Passports: a sharp, flat photo of the photo page works, but pasting the two MRZ lines is faster and 100% reliable — the tool validates them against ICAO check digits.
• Born-digital PDFs (with a real text layer) compare instantly; scanned PDFs and photos are run through OCR, which takes a few seconds per page.

Not legal advice or a filing tool. Casefile is a review aid — it highlights differences for a human to assess. It does not transmit, store, or submit anything. Verify all findings against the source documents.
